# ☀️ Solar Energy Harvesting System

## 📌 Overview

The **Solar Energy Harvesting System** is an IoT-based project designed to capture solar energy using photovoltaic panels and store it efficiently for powering electronic devices.
This project demonstrates how renewable energy can be utilized to create sustainable and energy-efficient systems.

The system collects energy from sunlight, converts it into electrical power, and stores it for later use. It can be used for small-scale power applications and educational purposes.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* To harvest renewable energy using solar panels.
* To store generated energy efficiently in batteries.
* To demonstrate an eco-friendly power generation system.
* To promote the use of sustainable energy technologies.

## 🚀 How the System Works

1. Solar panels capture sunlight and convert it into electrical energy.
2. The generated energy is regulated using a voltage regulator.
3. The power is stored in a rechargeable battery.
4. The stored energy can be used to power electronic devices.
